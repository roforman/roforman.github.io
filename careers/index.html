Hiring Process

  1. 1

    Application

    Send your resume and a short introduction to info@roforman.com (portfolio / GitHub link recommended for engineering roles).

  2. 2

    Screening Interview

    We review background, role fit, and project experience. Some roles may include a short technical discussion.

  3. 3

    Technical / Team Interview

    Role-specific interview with team members covering problem-solving, implementation experience, and collaboration style.

  4. 4

    Offer & Onboarding

    Successful candidates receive an offer and onboarding plan with role expectations and initial project scope.
